About palakol

Palakollu, commonly known as Palakol, is a town in the West Godavari district of the Indian state of Andhra Pradesh. It holds historical and cultural significance and is known for its temples, educational institutions, and agricultural activities.

Some Cool Stories

The town is renowned for its ancient temples, including the famous Ksheera Rama Lingeswara Swamy Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Other notable temples include the Venugopala Swamy Temple and the Datta Sai Temple.

Palakol celebrates various cultural and religious festivals with great fervor. The most significant festival is the Maha Shivaratri, which attracts devotees from all over the region. Other festivals like Diwali, Navratri, and Ugadi are also celebrated with enthusiasm.
